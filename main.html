How Does Our UberEats Clone Works?

With the objective of the launch and manage the food delivery business within a matter of days, Our UberEats clone includes an adaptive workflow that allows the customers to place the order effortlessly. Here's how our UberEats clone app works:

1.

USER REGISTRATION

Initially, the customers download the app and register their presence via email, mobile numbers, and social media profiles. These actions are also extended to both restaurant owners and delivery executives to make their online presence consistent.

2.

SEARCH FAV MENU OR RESTAURANT

After the registration process, customers can easily search the restaurants as per their familiarity and the food quality. Then, the customer searches their menus, adds their items to the cart.

3.

PLACE ORDER

After the deep analysis of the restaurants and the food available, the customers can place the food orders instantly.

4.

INSTANT FOOD ORDER PICKUP

They either accept or reject the pickup requests based on their active state. Also, they send periodic notifications to the customers regarding the trip of orders consistently.

5.

TRACK YOUR ORDER IN REAL-TIME

With the built-in feature called GPS and map-based navigation, the customers can easily track the food orders till they arrive.

6.

GET YOUR ORDER AT DOORSTEP

Delivery executives finally delivered the ordered meals to your doorstep. If you select the COD option in payment mode selection, then you can hand over the cash to the delivery executives directly.
